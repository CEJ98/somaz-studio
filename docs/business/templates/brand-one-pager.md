# Brand One-Pager

## Somaz Studio

Architecture-led design for residential, hospitality, and development projects.

Somaz Studio combines architecture, interiors, visualization, and partner-led local compliance for clients working across Argentina, the US, and international markets.

## What Somaz does

- architectural direction and phased design scope
- interior architecture and material definition
- visualization for approvals, investor decks, and pre-construction clarity
- technical coordination and partner-led compliance when required

## Who Somaz is for

- developers with early or mid-stage projects
- boutique hospitality operators
- premium residential clients with clear goals
- architects who need stronger design support or presentation packages

## Why clients choose Somaz

- architecture-led judgment instead of image-only production
- clear packages and phased delivery
- global remote workflow with commercial discipline
- local licensed partner coordination when jurisdiction requires it

## Delivery model

- Argentina: broader architectural scope where licensure and jurisdiction allow it
- US / international: Somaz leads design and coordinates with licensed local partners when sign-off, adaptation, or submission is required

## Positioning line

Somaz does not compete with low-cost renderers or generic design freelancers. It sells design direction, spatial clarity, and the right package to move a project forward.
