# Sofía Short Bio

Sofía Mazzucco is the founder of Somaz Studio, an architecture-led studio based in Miami with operational depth in Argentina and a global remote delivery model. Trained as an architect in Argentina, she leads residential, hospitality, and development work that combines design direction, interiors, visualization, and coordination with local licensed partners when projects require sign-off or submission in external jurisdictions.

Her work focuses on giving projects clarity early: stronger concepts, sharper spatial decisions, and deliverables that align clients, developers, consultants, and local partners around the same direction.
