# Brand Positioning

## Core position

Somaz Studio is an architecture-led studio for residential, hospitality, and development projects. The studio combines design direction, interiors, visualization, and partner-led local compliance for international delivery.

## What Somaz represents

- architectural direction, not decoration
- premium precision over volume
- calm, structured international delivery
- architecture, interiors, and visualization under one logic
- clear packages, clear scope, clear next step

## Primary audience

- small and mid-sized developers
- boutique hospitality operators
- premium residential clients with a defined project
- architects or studios that need strong design support

## Who to filter out

- price shoppers comparing Somaz to low-cost renderers
- clients asking for signed plans in markets where Somaz is not the local licensed firm
- projects with no brief, no budget, and artificial urgency
- open-ended creative work with no scope discipline

## Differentiation

### Against local firms

Somaz sells design direction, clarity, and agile international coordination rather than physical office scale or local firm infrastructure.

### Against freelancers

Somaz sells a studio-grade process: phased scope, QA, deliverable discipline, and the ability to coordinate partners and consultants when the work requires it.

### Against visualization studios

Somaz does not start from the image. It starts from the project. Visualization is used to define, align, and advance the project rather than to stand in for architectural thinking.

## Tone

- sober
- precise
- architecture-led
- commercially adult
- technical without empty jargon

Avoid:

- luxury cliches
- vague aspirational language
- overpromising local licensure
- presenting photoreal renders as the full identity of the studio

## Message pillars

- design direction
- spatial clarity
- commercial readiness
- local compliance through the right partner

## Approved language

- architecture-led design
- full-scope architecture in Argentina
- interiors and visualization for residential, hospitality, and development
- local licensed partner coordination
- permit and presentation packages prepared for local review and adaptation when required

## Avoid

- licensed architectural firm in Miami
- signed plans by Somaz in US markets
- full-service architecture everywhere
- design studio as the main umbrella without architecture
- photoreal renders as the primary brand promise

## Default tagline

Architecture-led design for residential, hospitality, and development projects.
